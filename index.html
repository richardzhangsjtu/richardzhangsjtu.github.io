location: Philadelphia, PA, the United States
tags: Bios

Hi there! Welcome to my personal blog! This is Abysswaltz, and you can simply call me my preferred name Richard (pretty similar to the pronounciation of my Chinese name). I’m currently a graduate student majoring in Mechanical Engineering, struggling to learn more about Mechatronic Systems and Computer Science to seek a relevant job. The blog’s website is based on Hexo(really friendly to those who are new to front-end web basics like me)[1] , and my blog contents are majorly divided into these parts:

  • Notes or codes that might come in handy from courses I’m taking(E.g. UPenn, Coursera, Udemy…)
  • Reviews on Games, Literature, Art…
  • Diaries & Reflections

Since I’m a bilingual speaker, most of my posts will be written in Chinese or English. Typically, posts related to learning are in English and reflections on art or events will be written in Simplified Chinese. Apologize for any inconvenience resulted from this!

Special thanks to jerryc127[2]‘s contribution to theme Buttefly [3] . Otherwise, this blog’s contents could be way too boring with the dull original theme.

Thank you again for reading this brief introduction. Hope you can enjoy my posts!
